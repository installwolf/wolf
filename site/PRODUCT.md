# PRODUCT.md — Wolf

register: brand

## Product purpose

Wolf is a macOS website blocker built as a **commitment device** against porn /
compulsive use. Its whole point is asymmetry: adding a site is instant; getting
past it is deliberately, structurally hard (a 48-hour cooldown, or a passphrase
only an accountability partner holds). A root watchdog self-heals any tampering;
an on-device content filter holds even against DoH, iCloud Private Relay, and
VPNs. Sold as an annual subscription: **Lone Wolf $29/yr** (one Mac),
**The Pack $99/yr** (family, multiple Macs).

Domain: installwolf.com · CLI: `wolf` · Platform: macOS.

## Users

Men (mostly) fighting a porn habit who have already tried the soft blockers and
talked their way past every one of them at a weak moment. Technical enough to
run a Mac app; done with being lied to by "100% unbypassable" marketing. They
want something that treats them like an adult making a hard commitment, not a
patient to be coddled or a sinner to be shamed.

## Tone & voice

Blunt, honest, tough-love. No shame, no euphemism ("porn," not "unwanted
content"), no wellness cooing, no fake absolutes. The brand's signature move is
**radical honesty**: it tells you exactly what it can and cannot do, because the
users have been burned by tools that oversold. Confident and a little feral, but
the promise is calm: a steadier, more directed life on the other side.

Think: a straight-talking friend who's been through it, not a therapist and not a
drill sergeant.

## Anti-references (do NOT look like these)

- Calm / Headspace wellness pastels, soft teal, rounded everything.
- Clinical recovery-app blue-and-white, stock "hopeful sunrise" photography.
- Alarm-red "PROTECT YOUR FAMILY" fear-marketing / church-filter aesthetic.
- Generic dark-SaaS-with-one-gradient landing page.
- Editorial-magazine lane (display-serif + italic + ruled columns).

## Strategic principles

- **Honesty is the differentiator.** A whole section admits the limits. That's
  the trust play, and no competitor does it.
- **Asymmetry is the product.** Every surface reinforces: easy in, hard out.
- **Privacy is a feature.** Everything runs on-device; nothing about your
  browsing leaves your Mac. Say so plainly.
- **The wolf guards the door.** The motif is a loyal, unrelenting sentinel that
  won't let you talk your way past it, not a monster.
